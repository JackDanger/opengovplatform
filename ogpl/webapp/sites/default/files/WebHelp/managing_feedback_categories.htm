Managing Feedback Categories

The feedback category is necessary for the correct assignment of the feedback to the Program Management Office, Point of Contact, or VRM Administrator. You must specify a feedback category for each feedback that appears in the feedback list, based on the content of the feedback. For example, when you receive a feedback that is related to the OGPL Web site, you may categorize that feedback under the Website Related category. You can also add, edit, delete, and track a feedback category.
