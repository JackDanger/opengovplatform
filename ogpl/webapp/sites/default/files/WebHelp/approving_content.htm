Approving Content

If the content, which the Content Creator sent for review, is appropriate for publishing on the OGPL Web site, you can approve the content. After you approve content, the content goes to the Publisher for review and approval.

While approving the content, you can also view the workflow history for that content. The Workflow History section displays details, such as the current workflow state for this content, when the content was created, when you send the content back to the Content Creator, when the content was sent to a Publisher for approval, or how many times the content has gone back for updates.

To approve content:

  1. In the navigation menu, click the Home link.

  2. In the Content List section, click the Approve this record icon for the content that you want to approve.
    A page that displays the title of the content that you selected appears.

  3. In the Change Content Management state section, click Publisher Review.

  4. In the Comment box, enter any additional information that you want to share with the Publisher.

  5. Click Submit.
    The content is approved and submitted to the Publisher.
