Managing Mini Panels

Mini panels serve as containers for content to be published on the OGPL Web site. A Mini panel can have several pieces of content within it, and each piece can have its own layout styles. Mini panels also provide a predefined collection of layouts for the content. A layout might have two columns, a header, footer, and three columns in the middle, or even a stack of several mini-panels. You can add a mini panel inside another, or create a mini panel with various pieces of content, and then put it inside a panels-page or panels-node.
