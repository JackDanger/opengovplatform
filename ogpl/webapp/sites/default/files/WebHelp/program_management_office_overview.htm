Program Management Office Overview

The Program Management Office (PMO) is a designated official who is responsible for implementing the feedback which is assigned by the VRM Administrator.

The PMO can perform the following tasks for each feedback:

  • View the list of feedback assigned by the VRM Administrator.
  • Update the feedback action for a feedback.
  • Reply to a feedback.
  • Forward a feedback to another Program Management Office  or Point of Contact.
  • Add notes to a feedback.
  • Revert a feedback to the VRM Administrator.
  • Assign an action status to a feedback.
  • Print feedback details.
  • View workflow history for feedback.

Notes:

  • The PMO cannot close or archive a feedback.
  • The PMO cannot perform any administrative tasks in the Open Government Platform Control Panel application.
