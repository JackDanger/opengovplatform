Viewing the Content List

When you log on to the application, the Content List section displays the content that the CMS Administrator, Content Creator or the Publisher has assigned for your approval. The most recently assigned content appears at the top of the list. The following table lists each column and its description.

Table: Description of columns in the Content List section

Column

Description

Title

Displays the name of the content record that the CMS Administrator, Content Creator or the Publisher has assigned for your review.

Type

Displays the content type, such as Page, Block, FAQ, Rotating Panel, Country Data and Feature Gallery.

Updated date

Displays the date when the content record was last updated.

Content Creator

Displays the username of the Content Creator who is assigned for this content.

Duration

Displays the time duration for how long the content has been in that workflow state.

Operations

Displays various tasks that you can perform to manage the content, such as:

  • Approve the content.

  • Send the content back into workflow.

  • Edit the content.

  • Preview the content.

Tip: Sorting the content list helps you quickly analyze, visualize, and understand various site content. By default, the content list displays the most recently updated content at the top of the list.  You can sort the content list according to various criteria, such as the content title, type, updated date, content creator, or moderator. To sort the content list, click the appropriate column name.
