Deleting Content

When you delete content, it is deleted from the OGPL Web site and the Content Management System. You cannot restore deleted content.

Note: You can delete content that is in Published status. If you delete content that is published on the OGPL Web site, the area where the content was displayed appears blank.

To delete content:

  1. In the Drupal menu, point to Content Management, Content, and then click List.
    The Content page appears.

  2. To search for the content that you want to delete, click the appropriate filter options.

  3. Under the Title column, select the check box for each content record that you want to delete from the OGPL Web site.

  4. In the Update options list, click Delete.
    The content is deleted from the OGPL Web site and the Content Management System.
