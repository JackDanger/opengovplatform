Reverting a Feedback

When you revert a feedback, the feedback is returned to the VRM Administrator who assigned the feedback to you, and the status of the feedback is updated to Reverted.

Note: You can only revert a feedback that is assigned to you.

To revert a feedback:

  1. In the navigation menu, click the VRM link.
    The VRM page appears.

  2. Optional: You can filter the feedback list. For more information, see Filtering the List of Feedback.

  3. In the row of the feedback that you want to reply to, click the Edit this record icon.
    The Feedback Details Edit page appears.

  4. Scroll down until you reach the end of the page, and then click Move to "Reverted"

The status of the feedback is updated to Reverted.
