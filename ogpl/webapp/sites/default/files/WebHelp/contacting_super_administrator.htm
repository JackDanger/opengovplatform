Contacting Super Administrator

You can send your queries, views, or suggestions to the Super Administrator of OGPL Web site. An e-mail message, containing the information that you provide, is sent to the Super Administrator.

To contact the Super Administrator:

  1. In the navigation menu, click Contact Admin.
    The Contact Admin page appears.

  2. Enter the appropriate information in each field. The following table lists each field and its description.

Table: Description of fields on the View page

Field

Description

Email

Displays your e-mail address.

Subject
(Required)

Enter the appropriate subject for the message that you want to send to the Super Administrator.

Message
(Required)

Enter the message that you want to send to the Super Administrator.

 

  1. Click Submit this form!
     An e-mail message, containing the information that you provided, is sent to the Super Administrator.
