Updating Items in RSS Feeds

You can update the feed items in an RSS feed by performing the following steps:

  1. In the navigation menu, point to Configurable Items, and then click Manage RSS Aggregator.
    The Feed Aggregator page appears.

  2. In the row of the RSS feed for which you want to update the feed items, under the Operations column, click the update items link.
    The feed items in the RSS feed are updated. The entry for this RSS feed under the Next update column displays the time duration remaining until the next update.
