Removing Items from RSS Feeds

You can remove the feed items from an RSS feed, by performing the following steps:

  1. In the navigation menu, point to Configurable Items, and then click Manage RSS Aggregator.
    The Feed Aggregator page appears.

  2. In the row of the RSS feed for from which you want to remove the feed items, under the Operations column, click the remove items link. A confirmation message appears.

  3. Click Remove items. All the feed items for this RSS feed are removed from the OGPL Web site.
