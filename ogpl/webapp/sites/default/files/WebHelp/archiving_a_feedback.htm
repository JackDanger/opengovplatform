Archiving a Feedback

If the reply that is sent by a feedback owner to the visitor who provided the feedback is unsatisfactory, and you do not want to reassign it to another owner, you can archive the feedback and reassign the feedback later. When you archive a feedback, the status of the feedback is updated to Archived and the feedback remains in the database.

Note: You can archive a feedback only when a feedback is in the Replied or the Reviewed state.

To archive a feedback:

  1. In the navigation menu, click the Home link.

  2. Click the All tab.
    A page that displays the list of all the feedback appears.
    – or –
    Click the status tab that corresponds to the current status of the feedback that you want to archive. For example, if you want to archive a feedback that is in Replied status, click the Replied tab.

  3. Optional: You can filter the feedback list. For more information, see Filtering the List of Feedback.

  4. In the row of the feedback for which you want to archive, click the Edit this record icon .

  5. The Feedback Details Edit page appears.

  6. Click Move to “Archive”.
    The status of the feedback is updated to Archived.

Tip: To reuse a feedback that is in the Archived state, update the status of the feedback to Assigned.
