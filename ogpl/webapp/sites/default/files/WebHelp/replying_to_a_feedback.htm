Replying to a Feedback

After a feedback is assigned to an owner, the feedback owner must send an e-mail reply to the visitor who provided the feedback. These replies are logged in the application so that the reply is available for further analysis or review.

Notes:

  • You can only reply to a feedback that is assigned to you.

  • You can reply to a feedback only when visitors provide their e-mail address while submitting the feedback.

To reply to a feedback:

  1. In the navigation menu, click the Home link.

  2. Click the My List tab .

  3. Click the Edit this record icon for the feedback that you want to reply to.
    The Feedback Details Edit page appears.

  4. Scroll down until you reach the end of the page, and then click Reply.
    The Create Feedback Reply page appears.

  5. You can compose a reply or use predefined text as a reply. The following table lists each field and its description.

Table: Description of fields in the Feedback Details Edits page

Title

Description

Mail to

(Required)

Displays the e-mail address of the visitor who provided this feedback.

CC

Displays your e-mail address.

Subject

(required)

Type an appropriate subject for the reply that you want to send for this feedback.

Pre-Formatted Response

Select an appropriate pre-formatted text as a reply to the visitor who provided the feedback.

Comments

(Required)

If you have selected a pre-formatted response, the text appears here. You can also type a reply to send to the visitor.
  1. Click Submit.

The feedback reply e-mail is sent to the selected recipients. The status of the feedback is updated to Replied.
