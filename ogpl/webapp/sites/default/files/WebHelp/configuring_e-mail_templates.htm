Configuring E-mail Templates

When a VRM administrator, PMO, or POC replies to a feedback or forwards a feedback, the recipient receives an e-mail notification. Similarly, the content creator receives an e-mail notification when any content on the OGPL Web site is about to expire. You can define various templates for these e-mail notifications.

You can use Drupal variables, also called tokens, in the subject and body of the e-mail template. When the e-mail is sent, these tokens are replaced with their values from the database. For example, the token [feedback_body] is replaced with the feedback text that the visitor provided through the OGPL Web site. You can view the list of Drupal tokens in the Replacement patterns section while creating or updating the e-mail template.
