Viewing the List of Feedback Received

You can view the list of feedback received from various sources of the OGPL Web site, such as the Contact Us page, Feedback page, Ratings page and Suggest Dataset page. The most recent feedback appears at the top of the feedback list. You can also view feedback details, such as the name of the sender, date, source, category, and current status of a feedback.

To view the list of feedback:

  1. In the navigation menu, click the Home link.
    The My List page appears.
    Note: When you click the Home link, the  My List view appears by default.

  2. To view the list of all feedback, click the All tab.
    The page that displays the list of all feedback appears.
     – or –
    Click the tab of the feedback status for which you want to view the list of feedback. For example, if you want to view the list of unassigned feedback, click the     Opened  tab.
