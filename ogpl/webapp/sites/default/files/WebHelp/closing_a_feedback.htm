Closing a Feedback

If the reply that is sent by a feedback owner to the visitor who provided the feedback is satisfactory, you can close the feedback. When you close a feedback the status of the feedback is updated to Closed and the feedback remains in the database.

Note: You can close a feedback only when a feedback is in the Replied or the Reviewed state.

To close a feedback:

  1. In the navigation menu, click the Home link.

  2. Click the All tab.

  3. A page that displays the list of all the feedback appears.
    – or –
    Click the status tab that corresponds to the current status of the feedback that you want to close. For example, if you want to close a feedback that is in Replied status, click the Replied tab.

  4. Optional: You can filter the feedback list. For more information, see Filtering the Feedback List.

  5. In the row of the feedback for which you want to archive, click the Edit this record icon .

  6. The Feedback Details Edit page appears.

  7. Scroll down until you reach the end of the page, and then click Move to “Closed”.
    The status of the feedback is updated to Closed.

Tip: To reuse a feedback which is in the Closed state, update the status of the feedback to Assigned.
