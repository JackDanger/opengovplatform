Sending Content for Approval

After updating the content that the CMS Administrator assigned, you must send the content to the Moderator for review and approval. While sending the content for approval, you can also view the workflow history for that content. The Workflow History section displays details, such as the current workflow state for this content, when the content was created, when you sent the content back to the Content Creator, when the content was sent to a Publisher for approval, or how many times the content has gone back for updates.

To send content for approval:

  1. In the navigation menu, click the Home link.

  2. In the Content List section, click the Send this record for approval icon  for the content record that you want to send for approval.
    The content page appears.

  3. In the Change Content Management state section, click Moderator Review.

  4. In the Comment box, enter the information that you want to share with the moderator.

  5. Click Submit.
    The content is sent to the Moderator for review.
